## How to sign

Contributions are accepted on an **inbound = this CLA** basis. You signal your
agreement in **either** of these ways (the project's `CONTRIBUTING.md` says which
is required):

1. **DCO sign-off (default for small changes):** add a `Signed-off-by:` line to
   every commit with `git commit -s`. The sign-off certifies the
   [Developer Certificate of Origin](https://developercertificate.org/) **and**
   your agreement to this CLA. Use your real name and the email on your commits.

2. **One-time CLA acknowledgement:** on your first pull request, comment:
   > I have read the CLA and I agree to it. — <your full name>, <date>

   (or sign via the CLA-assistant bot if one is configured on the repo).

Corporate contributors (contributing on behalf of an employer) must have an
authorized signatory complete a Corporate CLA — contact the Maintainer.
